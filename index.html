What's the Web's biggest problem?

Over 90% of web users in the UK are concerned about the ways that companies like Facebook, Google, and Amazon are amassing information about them. But, since we need to share and communicate, we keep using these "cloud services" anyway.

This needs to change. We shouldn't need to be spied upon to share.

So, we're developing a free and open source web platform called WebBox that enables a new class of apps that let you exchange data directly with your friends without any centralized servers -- meaning no Big Databases either.
