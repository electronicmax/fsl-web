What's the Web's biggest problem?

Over 90% of web users in the UK are concerned about the ways that companies like Facebook, Google, and Amazon are amassing information about them -- but without alternatives, keep using these "cloud services" anyway.

This needs to change.

Introducing WebBox

WebBox is a free and open source platform that will enable a new generation of serverless web apps that let information be exchanged directly between users. This means no centralized servers, no centralized administration, and no Big Databases either.

Developer Preview 1 is coming 29 Feb 2012.
