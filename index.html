What's the #1 problem with the Web today?

Over 90% of web users in the UK are concerned about the ways that companies like Facebook, Google, and Amazon are amassing information about them. But, since people need to share and communicate with one another, they keep using these "cloud services" anyway.

This needs to change. You shouldn't need to have Web sites spy on you to share information with your friends.

We're developing a free and open source web platform called WebBox that fosters the creation of a new class of web apps that let you exchange data directly with your friends (i.e. peer-to-peer) without any centralized servers -- meaning no Big Databases either.

Find out more about WebBox, a revolutionary open platform centered about you, not them -- and the next Person-Centric Web.
